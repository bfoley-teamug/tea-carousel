Tea History

Tea's history reaches back thousands of years

This site is dedicated to my love for tea. Tea is the second-most consumed beverage in the world behind water. It's been around for 2,000 years. Tea is the most popular manufactured drink consumed in the world, equaling all others – including coffee, chocolate, soft drinks, and alcohol – combined. Worldwide production is in the millions of tons annually. It's really healthy for body, mind, and soul. Tea culture and tradition are unmatched. There's nothing better than gathering with friends over tea. So lift a cup. Here's to tea!

I Love Tea

"Drink your tea slowly and reverently, as if it is the axis on which the world earth revolves." — Thich Nhat Hanh

Before I quit regular coffee-drinking 10 years ago, I didn't know that tea had so much to offer. Tea, to me, is a morning companion that is calming and invigorating at the same time. If I were to find myself stranded on a desert island, I truly hope I have my tea with me.
